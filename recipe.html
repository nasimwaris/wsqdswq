Simple sponge cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 8 gm swelf raising floor

note on Ingredients

Castor sugar Finely granulated white sugar
Self rainsing floor
A pre mixed combination of flour and leveling agents (usually baking powder).

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190c
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan
  3. In a medium a bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy
  4. Flod in flour
  5. Pour mixture into the prepaed pan
  6. Bake for 20min in the preheated oven
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack
